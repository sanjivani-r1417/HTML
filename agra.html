Agra

**Agra** is a historic city in Uttar Pradesh and one of India's most famous tourist destinations.
It is best known for the magnificent Taj Mahal, a white marble monument built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife.
Agra is also home to other important historical sites such as Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri.
The city reflects the rich heritage of the Mughal Empire through its architecture, culture, and traditions.
Visitors from around the world come to Agra to admire its historical monuments, learn about its history, and experience its vibrant local markets and cuisine.
