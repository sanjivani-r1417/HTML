Jammu & Kashmir

**Jammu** is a beautiful city in the union territory of Jammu and Kashmir and is often called the "City of Temples"
because of its many ancient and sacred temples. It is an important tourist and pilgrimage destination, attracting visitors from across India and abroad.
One of its most famous attractions is the Vaishno Devi Temple, one of the most visited religious sites in the country.
Jammu is also known for its scenic hills, historic forts, colorful markets, and rich cultural heritage. The city's pleasant surroundings, spiritual significance,
and natural beauty make it a popular destination for tourists seeking both relaxation and religious experiences.
