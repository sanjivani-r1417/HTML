Kerala - Munnar

Kerala is a beautiful state located in the southwestern part of India, often called “God’s Own Country” because of its stunning natural beauty.
It is famous for its calm backwaters, green landscapes, tea plantations, hill stations, and clean beaches. Major tourist attractions include places like Munnar,
known for its tea gardens and cool climate, Alleppey for its houseboat cruises, Kochi for its historical and cultural significance, Wayanad for its forests and wildlife
, and Kovalam for its popular beaches. Kerala is also well known for its rich culture, traditional dance forms like Kathakali, and delicious cuisine such as Kerala Sadya and seafood dishes.
The state attracts tourists from all over the world due to its peaceful environment, natural beauty, and unique cultural heritage.
