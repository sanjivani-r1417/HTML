Goa

**Goa** is one of India's most popular tourist destinations, located on the western coast along the Arabian Sea.
It is famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, water sports, and rich Portuguese heritage.
Tourists visit Goa to enjoy attractions such as sandy beaches, historic churches, forts, and colorful markets.
The state is also known for its delicious seafood, cultural festivals, and pleasant tropical climate. Popular places to visit include Baga Beach,
Calangute Beach, Fort Aguada, and Basilica of Bom Jesus. With its unique blend of natural beauty, adventure, and culture, Goa attracts millions of visitors every year.
